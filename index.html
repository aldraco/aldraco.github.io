Where did I come from?

I first learned to program for the web in college, using HTML and some light CSS. Though I initially did not intend pursue programming as a career, I dabbled with the code I knew and tried to learn a bit more. After realizing I could have a career doing something I already loved, I decided to jump in head first.

Where am I going next?

I aim to create pixel-perfect, robust, full stack solutions for companies and non profits. My social sciences and business background allows me to utilize my writing skills to create meaningful content, without which even the most cleverly designed websites cannot truly reach customers, clients, and stakeholders.
