The bread and butter of a web developer. I have had the opportunity to design a few websites that required minimal javascript, just a clean presentation of relevant information.

Samples

Some of my recent projects:

Church Website

The church needed a simple website that reflected both the warm, welcoming community and the noble, historic building they meet in.
A lightly-customized Wordpress installation coupled with a simple brochure site gives visitors information about the church, directions, and an idea of what's been going on recently -- all with a very low footprint.

Profile/Portfolio Site

The primary function of the portfolio site is to give work samples, some biographical information, and give potential clients and employers an easy way to contact me.

Web Apps

RecipeBox

This project is an opportunity to learn node, Express, and basic routing.

I am designing the app to allow users to maintain a personal recipe box, from which they can share recipes or keep them private. Users login via local authentication or Twitter.

This project is currently in a private repository, but will be hosted on Heroku as soon as it's done.
