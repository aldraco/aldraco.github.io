Time Coordinator App

node, Express, MongoDB/mongoosejs, Angular, Jade

This app is an open source project for Free Code Camp.

Finding availability for meetings when team members are in different time zones can be a challenge. Our app does the timezone conversion work for the user, letting everyone think in terms of their own time zone and preventing scheduling mistakes.

I am on a team of four Free Code Camp students who are building this app. You can see the repo here.

Open Source

Free Code Camp

node, Jade, MongoDB

FreeCodeCamp is an open source, non-profit, online bootcamp for motivated individuals to learn Javascript.

Campers who complete the requisite 800 hours of training are eligible to be assigned projects for real non-profit organizations, so they can both gain experience and give back to the community.

While I was waiting on being assigned to a project, I made some contributions to FCC's codebase:

  • MDN helper links for our algorithm practice
  • A feature to easily allow campers to pair program with each other
  • Various maintenance tasks (error handlers, bug fixes)

I also enjoy the opportunity to stream frequently for FreeCodeCamp on their Twitch channel. You can watch FCC campers code, live - everything from pair programming, bug fixes, and personal projects. Find us at http://twitch.tv/freecodecamp.
