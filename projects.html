Web Apps/Full Stack

Pinterest Clone

Node/express, socket.io, Angular, design

This app is a clone of Pinterest, but users instead save 'wins,' or things that are particularly amazing to their profile.

The front page and the 'Recent Wins' page both feature a socket connection to the server; whenever another user saves a new win, they appear live on your page. The back end is also smart enough to know when you delete a win from your profile that you re-blogged from someone else that it should just delete the reference, and not the whole item.

Amanda Jones and the Family Band

Country Music artist website

Angular, mobile-responsive design, Stylus, Facebook Graph API

http://acjonesmusic.com

Amanda Jones on Facebook

Amanda Jones, a rising country artist from Ohio, needed a website that allowed her fans to keep up with her busy touring schedule, as well as advertise her talent to producers and record labels.

The page uses Facebook's Graph API to coordinate her events calendar, eliminating the need for Amanda to enter data into yet another website. As much of the content as possible is automated and curated from her social media presence, allowing Amanda to focus on doing what she loves - singing and playing energetic shows.

The site is fully mobile-friendly, allowing fans to visit her site while at a show without missing any content.
