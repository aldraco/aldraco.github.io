About Me

I am grateful to say that I live in Vermont, a beautiful state with lots of unspoiled natural beauty. When I'm not behind the computer, I try to enjoy as much of it as I can.

Personal Interests

Outdoors

I love to be outside. When you work on a computer, getting a chance to breathe some fresh air and move around is a treat.

I like to run, hike, and swim. I try to train for a race as often as I can, just to stay in shape. In winter, my husband and I enjoy alpine skiing and snowshoeing.

Travel

I also love to travel, though unfortunately I realized this a little later than I would have liked.

My most recent adventures were in Vienna and Innsbruck, Austria, Montreal and Quebec City, Canada.

Cooking

I am gluten intolerant, but the restriction has only served to expand my tastes and cooking abilities. Sometimes a little guidance is all you need to discover a new favorite meal.

I particularly enjoy ethnic food, and usually am the one cooking it at our house. If I had one complaint about Vermont (and it would only be one!), it would be a lack of a "real" Mexican restaurant.

When I was in Vienna, I also ended up taking lots of pictures of coffee and cake!

Career Interests

Full Stack Web Development

I am most interested in doing development across the stack. Finding out how all of the puzzle pieces fit together and making them work in tandem is highly satisfying. I am strongest in Node and Express, and working on Angular to round out my knowledge of the MEAN stack.
